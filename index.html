Approach

How I work.

Step 01

Understand the actual landscape

Before anything else, I want to know what kind of market you're in, what you're up against, and where the real pressure points are. Most bad strategy starts with skipping that part.

Step 02

Build work that can last

Whether it's links, coverage, positioning, or content direction, the goal is the same: work that still makes sense six months from now, not just something that looks good in a report next week.

Step 03

Keep it honest

Some things move the needle and some don't. I'd rather be clear about that than dress up weak results or pretend every activity matters equally.
